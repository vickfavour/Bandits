🌙 Why Join Us?

Are you tired of following the rules? Do you dream of moonlit escapades and secret hideouts? The Shadow Brotherhood isn't just a band of outlaws—we're a family. A slightly dysfunctional, definitely wanted family.

We offer:

  • Free training in stealth, swordplay, and sarcasm
  • A share of all "liberated" treasures
  • The best campfire stories this side of the kingdom
  • Matching cloaks (dry-cleaning not included)

📜 The Legend of the Brotherhood

It all began on a stormy night ten years ago, when a group of misfits—an ex-knight, a disgraced bard, a thief with a heart of gold, and a guy named Steve—decided that the kingdom's laws were more like... suggestions.

What started as a petty theft of the Duke's favorite cheese wheel evolved into the most notorious bandit crew in the realm. We've robbed carriages, outsmarted sheriffs, and once accidentally saved a village from a dragon (long story, ask at the interview).

Our hideout? A secret location known only to members. Our motto? "No one gets left behind—unless they're slowing us down, then we at least leave a map."

🏆 Infamous Heists

The Great Cheese Caper (Year 1): Stole 400 wheels of aged cheddar. The Duke still cries at dinner parties.

The Royal Sock Swindle (Year 3): Replaced the King's entire wardrobe with itchy wool. Hilarity ensued.

The Treasury Tango (Year 5): Our most ambitious job. We didn't get the gold, but we did get away with the treasurer's favorite quill. Moral victory.

Operation: Free the Chickens (Year 7): Liberated 200 chickens from Baron Von Cluck. They now roam free in the northern hills.

THE CODE OF THE ROAD
Break these, and you're walking home. Without your boots.
  • Never steal from the poor. We're bandits, not monsters. The poor have nothing worth taking anyway.
  • Share the loot equally. Except Steve. Steve gets 5% less because of the incident with the donkey.
  • No betraying the crew. Snitches get stitches. And possibly dropped in a well.
  • Always have an escape plan. And a backup plan. And a backup to the backup.
  • Help a fellow bandit in need. Even if you owe them money. Especially if you owe them money.
  • Never sing during a heist. Looking at you, Ned.
  • Respect the hideout. Clean up after yourself. The rats are employees, not roommates.
  • Always buy the first round. After a successful job. Before a job is just showing off.

⚖️ The Three Trials

Every recruit must pass three trials to earn their cloak:

Trial of Shadows: Sneak past the sleeping bear in the east cave. (Don't worry, he's mostly deaf.)

Trial of Wits: Out-haggle Granny Loaf at the market. (No one has succeeded, but trying is mandatory.)

Trial of Loyalty: Take the secret oath while standing on one foot in a bucket of pudding. Tradition is tradition.
